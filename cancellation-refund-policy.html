Cancellation & Refund Policy

Last updated: January 2024

Zero Risk Guarantee: At Growth Magnet Studio, we operate on a performance-based model where you only pay for closed jobs. This means there are no upfront costs to cancel or refund.

1. Our Performance-Based Model

Growth Magnet Studio operates exclusively on a pay-per-performance model for our AI hybrid appointment system and lead generation services. This means:

  • No setup fees or upfront costs
  • No monthly subscription fees
  • Payment only occurs when you successfully close a job from our leads
  • Zero financial risk to start our services

2. Service Cancellation

2.1 Cancellation by Client

You may cancel our services at any time with the following conditions:

  • 30-day written notice: Provide written notice via email to terminate services
  • No cancellation fees: There are no penalties or fees for canceling our services
  • Outstanding obligations: Any commissions owed for closed jobs prior to cancellation remain due
  • Lead pipeline: Leads already in progress will continue to be serviced until completion

2.2 Immediate Cancellation

Services may be canceled immediately in the following circumstances:

  • Failure to meet our 30-day guarantee of 5 pier installation appointments
  • Breach of service agreement by Growth Magnet Studio
  • Mutual agreement between both parties

3. Refund Policy

3.1 Commission Refunds

Since payment is only made after successful job completion, refund scenarios are limited but may include:

  • Disputed job closure: If a job is later determined to be invalid or not properly closed
  • Lead quality issues: If a lead was misrepresented or did not meet agreed-upon qualification criteria
  • Service failure: If we fail to deliver services as outlined in the agreement

3.2 Refund Process

When a refund is warranted:

  1. Submit a written refund request within 60 days of the disputed transaction
  2. Provide documentation supporting the refund claim
  3. Allow up to 10 business days for investigation
  4. Approved refunds will be processed within 5-7 business days

4. Service Guarantee

30-Day Guarantee

We guarantee 5 qualified pier installation appointments within your first 30 days of service. If this guarantee is not met, you may:

  • Cancel services immediately with no obligations
  • Extend the trial period for an additional 30 days
  • Receive priority support to improve lead quality

5. What Happens After Cancellation

5.1 Data and Information

  • Your business information will be removed from our active systems
  • Lead data will be retained for 90 days for final reconciliation
  • Performance analytics will be provided upon request

5.2 Outstanding Obligations

  • Final invoice for any closed jobs will be sent within 30 days
  • Payment terms remain as agreed (typically 30 days from invoice)
  • Any disputes must be raised within 60 days of final invoice

6. Circumstances Beyond Our Control

Service may be suspended or canceled due to:

  • Changes in advertising platform policies
  • Technical issues with third-party services
  • Legal or regulatory changes affecting our services
  • Force majeure events

In such cases, no penalties apply to either party, and services will resume when possible.

7. Reactivation of Services

Former clients may reactivate services at any time:

  • No reactivation fees apply
  • Previous performance data may be referenced
  • New service agreement may be required
  • Same performance guarantees apply

8. Dispute Resolution

For any cancellation or refund disputes:

  1. Contact our support team first for resolution
  2. Escalate to management if needed
  3. Mediation through agreed-upon third party
  4. Binding arbitration as final resort

9. Contact for Cancellations

To cancel services or request a refund, contact us at:

Growth Magnet Studio
Email: support@growthmagnetstudio.com
Subject Line: "Service Cancellation Request" or "Refund Request"
Website: growthmagnetstudio.com

10. Policy Updates

This policy may be updated periodically. Changes will be communicated to active clients and posted on our website. Continued use of services after policy updates constitutes acceptance of the new terms.

Remember: Our zero-risk, pay-per-performance model means you have nothing to lose by trying our services. We're confident in our ability to deliver results for your foundation repair business.
